Skis-Manager is a comprehensive application for managing your ski equipment and mountain outings.

Main features:

EQUIPMENT MANAGEMENT
• Record all your skis with their characteristics (brand, model, size, radius, waist, type)
• Manage your ski boots (brand, model, size, flex, shell length)
• Link your skis and boots to users
• Customizable equipment brand database
• Categorize your skis (Piste, Freeride, Race, Slalom, GS, Surf, Skating, Touring, etc.)

OUTING TRACKING
• Detailed outing log with date, user, equipment used
• Outing types: Piste, Off-piste, Race, Training, Touring
• Record friends present and off-pistes done
• Options for rental or loaned equipment
• Complete history with filters by user or ski pair

EQUIPMENT MAINTENANCE
• Maintenance operations tracking (sharpening, waxing, repairs)
• Automatic alerts: number of outings since last sharpening/waxing
• Complete maintenance history

OFF-PISTE MANAGEMENT
• Create your favorite off-piste library
• Rate each descent
• Record conditions (powder, rocks, etc.)
• Statistics: number of runs per off-piste

MULTI-USER
• Manage multiple user profiles (family, friends)
• Individual statistics for each skier
• Record who you ski with

SEASON STATISTICS
• Global view: total number of outings, off-pistes
• Per user: detailed statistics
• Per ski pair: usage of each pair
• Maintenance tracking per pair

BACKUP AND SYNC
• Embedded local SQLite database
• Optional WebDAV synchronization
• Database export/import
• No connection required to use the app
• All your data remains under your control

PRIVACY
• No trackers
• No ads
• No account required
• Works completely offline
• 100% free and open source

The application supports automatic light/dark mode according to system preferences.
